Occupational therapy
and Speach pathology

Our Occupational Therapist is experienced in providing support for consumers with psychosocial disability and children with special needs. Mental health illnesses can interfere with or make doing any of the following difficult: • taking care of yourself and/or family • participating in paid or unpaid work • enjoying your leisure time, e.g. hobbies, sports, spending time with friends You may find that you need support to maintain your independence and your satisfaction with everyday activities. Working as part of a mental health multi-disciplinary team, occupational therapists can provide you with this support.
